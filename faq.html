FAQ

Here we answer some common questions people have about us

Q

How does the lighthouse accept people for consultations?

After the appointment you can apply for as many meetings as you want. There is no differentiation between situations and all the problems discussed in the consultations is absolutely confidential so you can rest assured there is security in your struggle.

Q

Does the lighthouse support only grieving children?

The lighthouse supports grieving children as the number 1 priority but also provides some closure for the parents and guardians of the children's parents. At the lighthouse we don't turn anyone away who is dealing with grief. Support is for everyone at the lighthouse and not just children.

Q

Do i need to have any specific skills to volunteer for the lighthouse?

There are no prerequisites to applying for a volunteer form. The only mandatory thing before volunteering is to have a police check up before finishing the form.
