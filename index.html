LIGHTHOUSE

Guiding families on their journey to healing.

Donate

Grief is a natural reaction to the loss of a loved one.
The lighthouse recognizes its impact, and gives healing time in an otherwise fast paced world.it’s a place more about beginnings than endings, more about celebration than sorrow, more about bright futures than the dark past.

What We Do

The Lighthouse gives children the sense of self back at a time when their lives have been turned upside down and inside out.

What We Offer

The Lighthouse is a place to reflect, recover, and re-adjust. If handled with caring and support, the death of a loved one can become a process of growth.

How It Works

Through our community, children can explore their difficult feelings, developing healthier forms of expressing their grief.
