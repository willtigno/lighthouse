Donate

At lighthouse any and all help is welcome. We have many children coming in on a daily basis who require assistance with many different types of situations. The lighthouse staff will provide unconditional support for children and what they would like to accomplish to better their emotional and mental state until they are past the point of grief. Helping the lighthouse means you will be directly influencing the lives of these children for the better,whether it be in our facilities or during an event.

Donate Methods

Email
Any email transfers can be mailed to our official email info@grievingchildrenlighthouse.org .

Cheque
Cheque donations should be mailed to 2522 Rebecca Street, Oakville, L6L 6N8.

Credit Card
You can also provide monthly donations which can amount to anywhere between 10$ to 100$ depending on your choice.
